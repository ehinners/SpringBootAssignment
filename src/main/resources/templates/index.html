Welcome!

Originally developed by Richard Garfield in 1993, and released by Wizards of the Coast, Magic The Gathering is THE classic trading card game. This website will walk fresh players, step by step, how to play this all-time-great fantasy based game. Moving forward, keep in mind the words "spell" and "card" may be used interchangably, the word "library" may refer to your deck, and "casting" a card means the same as playing a card.

As you encounter more cards, you may find cards that bypass rules such as once per turn limits. Remember: Reading The Card, Explains The Card
